Ok, so you chose to draw the face for your first go.
Just a warning, this is not for the faint of heart.
I'm just kidding but this is going to be somewhat difficult.



So lets start, first you draw a circle (odviously) and in the middle you draw a line going down.
Just remenber that the end of that line will become your chin later.





Next yoy want to draw a sort of tall U, this is going to be the jaw/mouth area.
Remember the bottom will link with the line you previously made.





The third step is to draw a curved line across the circle.
You then start adding two boxes. These will act as your eyes.
Then you mark where you want your mouth and nose
