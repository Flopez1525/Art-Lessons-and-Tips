Colors: what are they?

Colors make up everything that you see and come in a wide variety too. The main ones are Red, yellow, and Blue. They are usually reffered to as the Primary colors. They make up every other color, the second colors in this line are called Secondary colors. Secondary colors consist of Purple, Green, and Orange.
